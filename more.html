Best Places to Stay

Hotel Danieli

Overlooking the iconic Grand Canal, Hotel Danieli exudes timeless luxury and Venetian charm. Housed within historic palaces dating back to the 14th century, this lavish accommodation boasts opulent decor, Michelin-starred dining options, and unparalleled views of Venice's landmarks. With its central location near St. Mark's Square, guests can easily explore the city's cultural treasures before retreating to their elegant rooms or suites.

Belmond Hotel Cipriani

Situated on the tranquil island of Giudecca, Belmond Hotel Cipriani offers a secluded retreat from the bustling streets of Venice. Surrounded by lush gardens and overlooking the lagoon, this five-star oasis features luxurious accommodations, a private pool, and exquisite dining experiences. Guests can indulge in gourmet cuisine at the Michelin-starred Oro Restaurant or unwind with a cocktail at the iconic Cip's Club, all while enjoying panoramic views of the Venetian skyline.

Ca' Sagredo Hotel

Set within a meticulously restored 15th-century palace along the Grand Canal, Ca' Sagredo Hotel embodies Venetian elegance and heritage. Each room and suite is adorned with frescoed ceilings and antique furnishings, creating a romantic ambiance reminiscent of a bygone era. Guests can savor traditional Venetian cuisine at the hotel's elegant restaurant or admire priceless artworks displayed throughout the property. With its proximity to Rialto Bridge and other landmarks, Ca' Sagredo Hotel offers a quintessential Venetian experience.

JW Marriott Venice Resort and Spa

Located on its own private island in the Venetian Lagoon, JW Marriott Venice Resort and Spa offers a tranquil escape just a short boat ride away from the bustling city center. Surrounded by lush gardens and breathtaking views, this luxurious resort features spacious accommodations, world-class dining options, and a rejuvenating spa. Guests can unwind in style at the rooftop pool, explore the nearby islands, or take advantage of the resort's complimentary shuttle service to St. Mark's Square, making it an ideal base for experiencing the best of Venice.

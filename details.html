Best Places to Eat

Osteria alle Testiere

Tucked away in a quiet alley, this intimate eatery boasts a seasonal menu highlighting fresh seafood sourced directly from the nearby Rialto Market. Dishes are expertly crafted with a focus on Venetian tradition, accompanied by an impressive selection of regional wines. Reservations are a must for a true taste of Venetian culinary excellence.

Antiche Carampane

Known for its authentic Venetian cuisine and warm atmosphere, Antiche Carampane offers a menu filled with traditional delicacies, including delectable seafood pasta and tender squid ink risotto. The welcoming staff provides excellent recommendations, ensuring a memorable dining experience in the heart of Venice.

Trattoria da Fiore

Situated in the picturesque neighborhood of San Polo, Trattoria da Fiore is celebrated for its homely ambiance and flavorful Venetian dishes. From creamy polenta topped with fresh seafood to tender Venetian-style liver, each plate reflects the culinary heritage of the region. Visitors can savor local specialties while soaking in the charming surroundings of this beloved trattoria.

La Zucca

Nestled along the peaceful canals of Santa Croce, La Zucca offers a unique dining experience centered around seasonal vegetables. This vegetarian-friendly gem impresses diners with innovative dishes such as pumpkin flan and artichoke risotto, showcasing the versatility of Venetian cuisine. With its cozy interior and inventive menu, La Zucca promises a delightful culinary journey for discerning palates.
